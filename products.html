Immersive Technology for

Dentaverse offers specially designed VR headsets tailored for dental environments, paired with an extensive, highly customized VR content library.

Our VR Ecosystem

A seamless blend of purpose-built hardware and empathetic software.

VR Headset Solution

Dentaverse offers specially designed VR headsets tailored for dental environments. These headsets are:

  • Lightweight, comfortable, and adjustable for all age groups
  • Designed for use during dental procedures without interference
  • Easy for dental staff to operate and maintain
  • Hygienic and suitable for clinical environments

Customized Content Library

Our VR system includes a wide variety of immersive content designed for different patient needs:

  • Educational Videos: Explain procedures visually to reduce fear of the unknown.
  • Cartoons & Entertainment: Distract and relax children and anxious patients.
  • Relaxation Environments: Virtual beaches, forests, and waterfalls to promote calmness.
  • Music Therapy: Soft, calming audio designed to reduce stress.

  • Dental students and training institutions

For Dental Students:
