The Technology

VR Headset Solution

Purpose-built for dental environments — combining clinical precision with immersive patient comfort.

VR Headset Hardware

Dentaverse ELITE headsets are engineered specifically for active dental procedures — not repurposed consumer devices.

  • Lightweight, adjustable fit for all patient age groups
  • Designed for use during procedures without jaw or instrument interference
  • Easy for dental staff to operate and sanitise between patients
  • Disposable medical-grade hygiene covers, changed with every use
  • Clinically certified for dental environments — UK medical device compliant

Customised Content Library

Our VR system includes a constantly-growing library of immersive content, developed for different patient profiles and anxiety levels.

  • Educational Videos: Explain procedures visually — reducing fear of the unknown
  • Relaxation Environments: Virtual beaches, forests, and waterfalls promoting calmness
  • Cartoons & Entertainment: Engaging distraction for children and anxious patients
  • Music Therapy: Clinically curated calming audio to reduce procedural stress
  • Expert Reference Videos: Treatment and medical resource library for practitioners
