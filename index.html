FAQ

Everything You Need to Know

Have questions? We've answered the most common ones below. Still unsure? Reach out to us anytime.

What is Dentaverse Elite?

Dentaverse Elite is a premium VR distraction therapy system designed specifically for dental clinics. Patients wear a lightweight VR headset during their appointment and are transported to calming virtual environments, making the entire experience stress-free and comfortable.

Is the VR equipment hygienic and safe?

Absolutely. All headsets are fitted with disposable, medical-grade hygiene covers that are changed between every patient. The hardware meets UK medical device standards and is designed with clinical environments in mind.

Does VR distraction therapy actually work for anxiety?

Yes. VR distraction therapy for dental anxiety is backed by peer-reviewed clinical studies. Research shows significant reductions in perceived pain and anxiety. Many patients report their appointment feeling far shorter than it actually was — a sign the brain was genuinely engaged elsewhere.

Can children use Dentaverse Elite?

Yes! We offer age-appropriate content for children from age 6 upward. The environments are safe, engaging and designed to capture young imaginations — think magical forests, underwater kingdoms, and space adventures — making dental visits a treat rather than a fear.

How do I get Dentaverse Elite in my dental clinic?

Get in touch via our Contact page to arrange a free demonstration and consultation. We handle full end-to-end installation, staff training, and ongoing technical support so your team can focus entirely on patient care from day one.

Does it interfere with dental procedures?

Not at all. The headset is designed to allow full clinical access to the mouth and jaw areas. Dentists have reported that procedures often run more smoothly as patients keep still naturally when immersed in a VR environment.

What types of VR environments are available?

Our library includes 50+ environments: serene beaches, tropical forests, stargazing experiences, interactive nature scenes, guided meditation journeys, and more. New environments are added regularly based on patient feedback and psychological research.

Is there ongoing support after installation?

Yes. Every Dentaverse Elite installation includes an ongoing support package — hardware maintenance, software updates, new content drops, and a dedicated UK-based support line. We're with you every step of the way.

Can patients with claustrophobia use VR?

For most patients with claustrophobia, the open, expansive VR environments actually help by replacing the confined clinical space with wide-open virtual vistas. We recommend a brief acclimatisation during the initial fitting to ensure every patient is comfortable before the procedure begins.

Does Dentaverse Elite work for all dental procedures?

Yes. From simple check-ups and fillings to root canals, extractions, and implant procedures — Dentaverse Elite is effective across the full spectrum of dental work. Longer procedures often benefit the most, as deeper immersion builds progressively over time.
