The Science Behind It

Why VR Works for
Dental Anxiety

The brain cannot fully process two streams of vivid sensory experience simultaneously. By occupying the visual, auditory, and spatial cortex with a rich, immersive environment, VR effectively gates the perception of discomfort and clinical stimuli.

This is not distraction in the conventional sense — it is a clinically validated form of cognitive offloading, studied across over forty peer-reviewed papers in dental and surgical contexts.

Peer-Reviewed Efficacy

VR distraction therapy is backed by clinical trials demonstrating significant reduction in both perceived pain and procedural anxiety scores.

Shorter Perceived Time

Patients consistently report appointments feeling shorter than they were — a direct effect of deep immersion engagement.

Measurable Physiological Change

Studies record lower heart rate variance and reduced salivary cortisol in VR-assisted dental patients versus control groups.

The Patient Journey

Patient Process of Elite

A seamless three-stage workflow designed to support the patient before, during, and after every Dentaverse ELITE appointment.

Step 01

Pre-treatment Education

Before the procedure begins, patients are introduced to the Dentaverse system. The clinician selects an appropriate VR environment and the patient receives a brief, reassuring orientation — understanding what to expect and choosing their immersive world.

Step 02

The Main VR-assisted Procedure

The patient is fitted with the lightweight, hygienic VR headset and fully immerses in their chosen environment. Clinical sights and sounds are replaced with calming visuals and spatial audio. The dentist performs the procedure unimpeded while the patient remains calm and cooperative throughout.

Step 03

Post-treatment Guidance

After the procedure, the patient is gently guided back and provided with aftercare instructions. A brief digital wellness check-in captures feedback to personalise future VR sessions. Patients consistently report feeling the appointment was shorter and more comfortable than expected.
