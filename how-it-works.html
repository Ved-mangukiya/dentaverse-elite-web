Dentaverse ELITE utilizes "Cognitive Gate Control Theory." By saturating the visual and auditory pathways with peaceful stimuli, we effectively block anxiety-related signals from reaching the primary consciousness.

This results in higher patient cooperation, fewer procedural interruptions, and a significantly elevated clinic reputation.
