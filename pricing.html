Common Pricing Questions

Is there a free trial?

Yes — all plans start with a 30-day free demonstration period. No card required.

Can I change plans later?

Absolutely. Upgrade or downgrade at any time. Changes take effect from the next billing cycle.

Are headsets replaced if damaged?

Yes. All plans include hardware replacement cover for accidental damage at no extra cost.

What's included in the annual discount?

Annual billing saves 20% versus monthly and locks in your current price for 12 months.
